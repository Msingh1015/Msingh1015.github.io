Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I make a booking?

A: You can make a booking by going to hotels tab in menu bar, select the hotel you want to book, click on book hotel and provide the required details. Alternatively you can see hotels in homepage which are giving offers.

Q: How do check if my booking is confirmed?

A: You can go to bookings tab in menu bar and view all your current and previous bookings.
